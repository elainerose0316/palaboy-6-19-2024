PALABOY: An Animated Shortfilm

by EY Fairies

Palaboy is an animated short film about a stray dog and its journey to survive daily life by scavenging for food and looking for shelter until it was adopted by an empathetic crew of a fast-food restaurant. The main objective of this film is to create an educational animated tale that will raise awareness about the current phenomenon of homeless animals and how to help them in a variety of methods.
